## Key Features

### Image Generation
19 best-in-class models including Flux 2 Pro, Recraft V4, Ideogram 3, Imagen 4, and Grok Imagine. 1–4 credits per image.

### Video Generation
45+ model variants across 15+ AI families: Kling 3.0 Pro, Veo 3, Runway Gen-4.5, Sora 2, Seedance 1.5. 8–24 credits per video.

### Cinema Studio
Professional cinematography with 12 cinema lenses, focal length control (8mm–50mm), aperture (f/1.4–f/11), and 6-axis camera movements.

### Dark Factory
Upload one product image. Select services. Generate everything automatically — photos, videos, UGC ads, social kits, and more.

### Node System
Visual workflow builder with 36 node types. Build custom pipelines, save templates, share to Marketplace.

### Intelligence Hub
Real-time market signals: Waves (trending content), Market Gaps, Content Ideas, and Monetization Blueprints.
