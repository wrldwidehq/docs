## Managing Your Plan

### Upgrade

Upgrade anytime from the Settings → Billing page:
- Immediate access to new features
- Prorated billing for the remainder of the period
- Credits from previous plan remain available

### Downgrade

Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period:
- Current features remain until period ends
- Downgraded features become unavailable after renewal
- Unused credits roll over (up to plan limit)

### Cancel

Cancel from Settings → Billing:
- Account remains active until period ends
- Export your data before cancellation
- Credits expire at period end

### Add Credits

Need more credits mid-month?
- Purchase credit packs from Settings → Billing
- Credits never expire while subscribed
- Credits roll over month-to-month (up to 2x plan limit)

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans?">
    Yes. Upgrade takes effect immediately. Downgrade takes effect at the end of your billing period. Prorated billing applies for upgrades.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do credits expire?">
    Monthly credits reset at the start of each billing period. Purchased credit packs never expire while you remain subscribed. Credits roll over up to 2x your plan's monthly allocation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are accepted?">
    We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual invoices. Stripe handles secure payment processing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    The Free plan is always available — no trial period, no credit card required. Create an account and start with 50 credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    We offer refunds for unused credits within 14 days of purchase, provided no content has been generated. Contact support with your request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's included in priority support?">
    Priority support means faster response times (typically under 4 hours on business days) and direct access to the support team via email.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
